Welcome to Pottery Palette!

Welcome to Pottery Palette, Little Rock’s newest creative hub for pottery lovers! We’re excited to bring a fun and inspiring space where you can unleash your inner artist. At Pottery Palette, you can choose from a wide selection of ready-to-paint clay objects, from charming mugs to unique figurines, all waiting for your personal touch. Our friendly staff is here to guide you through the painting process, ensuring you have a memorable experience. In addition to painting, we offer special pottery-making classes led by talented professionals, where you can learn the art of crafting your own clay creations. Whether you’re a seasoned artist or trying your hand at pottery for the first time, Pottery Palette is the perfect place to explore your creativity and have fun with friends and family. Come join us and make your masterpiece today!

Join for a family fun activity!

Join our creative community at Pottery Palette! Whether you're here for a fun night out with friends or a family bonding activity, our pottery painting sessions are perfect for all ages and skill levels. Let your imagination run wild as you bring your clay creations to life!

Pottery Painting

At Pottery Palette, you can choose from a variety of clay objects to personalize, including bowls, mugs, plates, and figurines. Our selection is designed to inspire your creativity, allowing you to paint each piece with your unique style. Once you’ve finished your masterpiece, we’ll carefully dry and package it, ensuring it is sent directly to your address for you to enjoy at home.
