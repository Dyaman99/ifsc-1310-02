Who We Are

Pottery Palette is a creative studio in the heart of Little Rock, Arkansas, where art meets community. We specialize in providing a hands-on pottery painting experience for people of all ages and skill levels. Whether you’re here to paint ready-made clay items, take a pottery-making class, or host a special event, our studio is designed to spark creativity and make lasting memories. With a welcoming atmosphere and expert guidance, we aim to be a space where everyone can relax, have fun, and create something uniquely their own.

Our Story

Pottery Palette began as a shared dream between two college friends, Daisy and Susan, who wanted to create something truly unique for their community. Daisy, an artist with a lifelong passion for creativity, had always dreamed of owning her own business. She envisioned a space where people could explore their artistic side, connect with others, and leave with something beautiful they created themselves.

While Daisy had the creative vision, she knew she needed help turning her dream into reality. That’s where Susan came in. With a background in business management and a knack for organization, Susan brought the structure and expertise needed to launch and grow their studio. Together, they combined their talents—Daisy’s artistic passion and Susan’s business skills—to create a space where creativity and fun come together.

Since opening Pottery Palette, Daisy and Susan have worked tirelessly to make the studio a place where everyone feels welcome, whether they’re painting their first mug, hosting a party, or learning the art of pottery-making. What started as an idea between friends has blossomed into a vibrant part of the Little Rock community, and the story is just beginning.

Meet the Founders

Daisy – Founder

Daisy is the creative heart behind Pottery Palette. Her lifelong passion for art and pottery inspired her to create a space where others could explore their creativity and make lasting memories. Since her college days, Daisy dreamed of owning her own business, one where she could combine her love for art with a fun, interactive experience for the community. With her artistic vision and hands-on approach, she’s made Pottery Palette a welcoming and inspiring studio for everyone to enjoy.

Susan – Partner

Susan is the business-savvy partner who helped turn Daisy’s dream into a reality. Having studied business management in college, Susan brought the expertise and structure needed to bring Pottery Palette to life. As Daisy’s close friend and confidante since their college years, Susan was the perfect person to help navigate the challenges of starting a business. Together, they’ve combined their skills to create a thriving studio that’s as fun as it is professional.

Why Choose Us?

We know there are many ways to spend your time, and we strive to make every visit to Pottery Palette a unique and enjoyable experience. Here’s why our studio stands out:

  • Creativity for Everyone: No experience needed! We provide all the tools and guidance to help you create something beautiful.
  • A Friendly Atmosphere: Our team is here to support and encourage you every step of the way.
  • Personalized Keepsakes: Every piece you create is truly one of a kind, and we take care of the glazing, firing, and delivery process to ensure it’s perfect.
  • Community Connection: From classes and workshops to private events, we’re proud to be a creative hub for Little Rock.
