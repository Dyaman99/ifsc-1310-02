Who We Are

Pottery Palette is a creative studio in the heart of Little Rock, Arkansas, where art and community come together. We specialize in offering a fun and welcoming space where anyone—whether a seasoned artist or a first-time creator—can explore the joy of pottery painting. From ready-to-paint clay objects to hands-on pottery-making classes, we’re here to inspire your creativity every step of the way.

Events

Pottery Making Class

December 20, 2024

Step into the art of pottery-making with our live pottery class, led by our talented instructor, Christine! Learn the basics of shaping and molding clay in this hands-on class, perfect for beginners and those looking to get creative on the pottery wheel. All materials are provided, so just bring your imagination and enjoy crafting your own unique piece!

Paint & Sip Nights

January 6 - March 30, 2025

Join us for a relaxing Paint & Sip Night! Bring your friends or make it a date night as you enjoy light refreshments and paint a pottery piece of your choice. Our team will be there to guide you, making it perfect for both beginners and seasoned painters.

Family Clay Day

January 12, 2025

Bring the whole family for a fun-filled afternoon of pottery painting! Kids and adults can pick from a variety of clay items to decorate, from animal figurines to colorful plates. Family Clay Day is a fantastic way to spend quality time together, with plenty of paint, creativity, and laughter.
